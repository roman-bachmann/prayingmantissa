Backtracing Innovation

From Snapchat’s Facial Recognition to the Inception of Semiconductors

Abstract

The US Patent & Trademark Office, short USPTO, offers a full-fledged directed graph of US patent citations since 1975. Many at the time obscure patents paved the way for groundbreaking new technologies in the future, as for example Google’s Page Rank (#6285999) or Apple’s touch screen devices (#7479949). In our project we endevor to visualize this massive graph, more than 89 Million edges, in an appealing and concise way in order to render the narration of more intricate stories possible.

Research questions

  • How to apply common graph algorithms to a massive data set?
  • How to efficiently travers and query a graph of that scale?
  • How to conveniently visualize a large and complicated graph?
  • How to draw conclusions about technology soley given patents and their citations?
